FAQ

Isn't this the dungeon entrance?

No. The entrance is the door by the executed ghost (68 moves). This lock is at Arlin's cell inside.

Do I need the key?

You can loot it from the warder by the cells. Or pick the lock - 25 moves, shorter than the entrance door.

How many moves?

25 moves (7 plates, 10 links).
