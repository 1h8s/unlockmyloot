FAQ

How is this different from the 2nd-floor lock?

One building, two locks: the 2nd floor has a different config; this one is the 3rd-floor storeroom. Rice-field guides often confuse these doors.

How do I reach the storeroom?

Rice Lord's key or a lockpick. The Rice Lord is a New Camp NPC overseeing the fields.

How many moves?

30 moves (5 plates, 9 links).
