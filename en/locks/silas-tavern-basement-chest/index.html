How to open the Silas tavern basement chest in Gothic 1 Remake

An underwater chest in the flooded basement under Silas's tavern - Bran's axe for the "Up to His Neck" quest. Don't confuse it with the storeroom chest behind the door past the bartender.

Where it is

What's inside

Bran's axe - required for the "Up to His Neck" quest.

FAQ

Isn't this the storeroom chest behind the bar?

No. Past Silas is a door to the tavern storeroom with a different chest on the right. This one is in the flooded basement below, underwater.

How do I get there?

Swim: underwater tunnel from the lake behind the tavern, or descend from the storeroom.

How many moves?

30 moves (5 plates, 10 links).
